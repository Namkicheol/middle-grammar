B. 일반동사의 현재형과 부정문


괄호 안의 단어를 활용하여 문장을 완성하시오. (단, 부정형은 축약형을 쓸 것)

1. I _______________ two sisters. We resemble each other. (have)

2. He _______________ three languages. He likes foreign languages. (speak) 

3. Kevin ______________ violin lessons on Mondays. He plays violin well. (take)

4. I __________________ in Incheon now. I live in Suwon now. (live)

5. Judy is a science teacher. She __________________ science. (teach)

6. Christina __________________ meat. She is a vegetarian. (eat)

7. My parents ________________ for a hospital. They are teachers. (work) 

8. The movie __________________ in five minutes. Let's hurry. (start)

9. Mia __________________ to school on foot. Her school is near her home. (go)

10. Ellie _________________ her house every day. Her house is clean. (clean)
