A. 동사의 목적어로 쓰인 to부정사


괄호 안의 단어를 활용하여 문장을 완성하시오. 

1. Andy wants _______________ a car. (have)

2. They promised ________________ the secret. (keep)

3. Sam wishes ______________ his own business. (start)

4. She decided _______________ the coffee machine. (buy)

5. Jamie hopes ______________ the project on time. (finish)

6. Sophie needs ______________ soon. (leave)

7. We agreed _______________ the new guidelines. (follow)

8. He wishes ________________ his English skills. (improve) 

9. You need _______________ your report by tomorrow. (submit)

10. My family planned _______________ to Australia this winter. (travel)

B. 조동사 will, should

괄호 안의 단어와 will 또는 should를 활용하여 문장을 완성하시오. 

1. Take care. I _______________ you tomorrow. (call)

2. They took a test today. They _______________ the result next Monday. (see)

3. You woke up late. From now on you _________________ to sleep early. (go)

4. Many people are waiting now. You ________________ in line, too. (wait)

5. It's raining hard. You _________________ at home. (stay)

6. The taxi is arriving soon. We _________________ home now. (leave)

7. Liley is 14 years old now. She _________________ 15 years old next year. (be)

8. Jiwon has a fever. She _________________ a doctor. (see)

9. We need to catch the early train. The train ________________ at noon. (depart)

10. The lesson starts soon. You _________________ off your cell phone. (turn)




정답

Point A
1. to have 2. to keep 3. to start 4. to buy 5. to finish 6. to leave 7. to follow 8. to improve 9. to submit 10. to travel


Point B
1. will call 2. will see 3. should go 4. should wait 5. should stay 6. should leave 7. will be 8. should see 9. will depart 10. should turn
