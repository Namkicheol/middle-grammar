B. something+형용사


우리말과 일치하도록 괄호 안의 말을 알맞게 배열하여 문장을 완성하시오.

1. 우리는 저녁으로 맛있는 것을 원한다. (tasty, something) 
→We want _______________________________________ for dinner.

2. Kelly는 디저트로 달콤한 것을 가져왔다. (something, sweet)
→ Kelly brought ____________________________________ for dessert.

3. 내가 너에게 중요한 것을 말해 줄게. (important, tell, something, you)
→ I will ________________________________________________________.

4. 제가 시원한 무언가를 마실 수 있을까요? (I, something, cool, have)
→ Can ____________________________________________________ to drink?

5. Betty는 무언가 재미있는 것을 보고 있다. 
(is, watching, interesting, something, Betty)
→ _______________________________________________________________

6. Sam은 Jenny를 위해 특별한 것을 준비했다. (something, prepared, special)
→ Sam ___________________________________________ for Jenny.

7. 그들은 부모님을 위해 멋진 것을 하기를 원한다. (to do, nice, something, want)
→ They ______________________________________________________ for their parents.

1. beautiful the sunset is 2. a talented singer she is 3. delicious the cake is 4. a fast runner Rick is 5. difficult this puzzle is 6. a clever student Nancy is 7. loud the music was 8. healthy this meal is
