A. 현재진행형과 현재진행형의 의문문


괄호 안의 단어를 활용하여 진행형 문장을 완성하시오. 

1. Lily _______________ the plants now. (water)

2. They _______________ coffee now. (drink)

3. The sunflower _______________ so fast. (grow)

4. We _______________ to our swimming lesson now. (go)

5. Tom _______________ to the post office now. (run)

6. The 333 bus ________________ now. (come)

7. The students __________________ French now. (learn)

8. The man _________________ the computer now. (fix) 

9. Olivia _________________ in the mirror now. (look)

10. My family _________________ dinner at a new restaurant now. (have)

B. 동명사


괄호 안의 단어를 알맞은 형태로 바꾸어 문장을 완성하시오. 

1. I kept _______________ for one hour for my friend. (wait)

2. Eric enjoys ______________, so he goes to a ski resort every winter. (ski)

3. Sarah gave up ______________ up early. (wake)

4. I finished ______________ a paper and went to sleep. (write)

5. Many Indian people avoid ________________ pork. (eat)

6. Laura practices ________________ for the audition. (sing)

7. The refrigerator suddenly stopped _________________. (work)

8. Amy enjoys _________________ a walk with her dog. (take)

9. She gave up _______________ Chinese. It was too difficult for her. (learn)

10. Would you mind _______________ off the light? (turn)
