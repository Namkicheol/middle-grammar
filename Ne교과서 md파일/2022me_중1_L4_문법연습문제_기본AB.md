(2) You should bring your umbrella on Tuesday. (T / F)
 (3) It will be cloudy on Wednesday. (T / F)
 (4) On Thursday and Friday, it will be sunny. (T / F)
 (5) You should wear a thick coat on the weekend because it will be snowy. (T / F)
