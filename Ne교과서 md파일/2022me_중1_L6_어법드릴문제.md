A. 감각동사 look+형용사

우리말과 뜻이 같도록 괄호 안의 단어와 동사 look을 사용하여 문장을 완성하시오.

1. 저 강아지는 배고파 보인다. (hungry)
→ That dog ____________ ___________.

2. 너는 그 사진 속에서 슬퍼 보인다. (sad)
→ ___________ ____________ _____________ in the picture.

3. Jasmine은 새로운 일에서 행복해 보인다. (happy)
→ ____________ ____________ _____________ with her new job.

4 학생들은 긴 수업 동안 지루해 보인다. (bored)
→ The students ____________ _____________ during long lessons.

5. 그 꽃들은 햇빛에서 아름다워 보인다. (beautiful)
→ The flowers __________ __________ in the sunlight.

6. 이 케이크는 다양한 색의 토핑으로 맛있어 보인다. (this, delicious)
→ ____________ ___________ ____________ ____________ with the colorful toppings.

7. 그 홀은 파티 후 지저분해 보인다. (messy)
→ The hall _____________ ____________ after the party.

8. 이 수학 문제는 어려워 보인다. (this, problem, difficult)
→ ___________ _____________ ____________ _____________ ______________.

B. 이유를 나타내는 접속사 because

보기에서 알맞은 말을 골라 접속사 because를 사용하여 문장을 완성하시오. 
보기 | she woke up late               she was full            the music was too loud               
it was cold outside    he had an important meeting     she forgot her swimsuit
       my dad's company moved there        we were expecting more guests


1. Eric turned down the volume _______________________________________.

2. She wore a coat ____________________________________.

3. Natalie missed the bus _____________________________________.

4. Joey left home early ________________________________.

5. We moved to a new city ______________________________.

6. She didn't eat cake ________________________________.

7. Mia didn't go swimming ______________________________.

8. We bought extra snacks _____________________________.





정답

Point A
1. looks hungry 2. You look sad 3. Jasmine looks happy 4. look bored 5. look beautiful 6. This cake looks delicious 7. looks messy 8 This math problem looks difficult

Point B
1. because the music was too loud 2. because it was cold outside 3. because she woke up late 4. because he had an important meeting 5. because my dad's company moved there 6. because she was full 7. because she forgot her swimsuit 8. because we were expecting more guests
