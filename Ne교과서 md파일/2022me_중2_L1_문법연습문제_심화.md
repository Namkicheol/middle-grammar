# 2022me 중2 L1 문법연습문제 심화

Lesson 1                                         2학년 ___반 ___번 이름: ___________
문법 연습문제
         심화
  두 문장의 뜻이 같도록 to/for/of를 이용하여 문장을 완성해 봅시다.

1 They passed the other guests the bread.
  → They passed ______________________________________________________________________.
2 Mom got us some tickets to the concert.
  → Mom got _________________________________________________________________________.
3 The guide gave the tourists a map of the city.
  → The guide gave __________________________________________________________________.
4 My brother made me a delicious sandwich for lunch.
  → My brother made _____________________________________________________ for lunch.


  다음 우리말과 같은 뜻이 되도록 괄호 안의 말을 이용하여 문장을 완성해 봅시다.

1 나는 밤하늘에서 밝게 빛나는 별을 보았다. (shines brightly)
  → I saw a star ___________________________________________________ in the night sky.
2 그는 결승골을 넣은 선수이다. (scored, the winning goal)
  → He is the player _________________________________________________________________.
3 이것은 내 여행 추억이 담긴 내 일기장이다. (has, all my travel memories)
  → This is my diary _________________________________________________________________.


  다음 문장에서 밑줄 친 부분을 바르게 고쳐 써 봅시다.

1 Our coach taught a new skill us.  
2 She showed her drawing for her parents.
3 We need to fix the machine who broke down last week.
4 The boy which forgot his homework had to stay after school.  
→ ____________  
→ ____________  → ____________  
→ ____________  



  다음 두 문장을 주격 관계대명사를 이용하여 한 문장으로 바꿔 써 봅시다.        

1 She made a song. It became very popular.
  → ______________________________________________________________________________________________
2 I met a man. He works for a fashion company.
  → ______________________________________________________________________________________________
3 The researchers built a robot. It can walk and talk.
  → ______________________________________________________________________________________________
