B. 목적격 관계대명사

  
 • 역할: 목적격 관계대명사는 두 절을 이어 주는 접속사 역할과 뒤에 나오는 절의 목적어를 대신하는 대명사 역할을 한다.
 • 형태: 선행사의 종류에 따라 who(m), which, that을 사용한다.

선행사의 종류
목적격 관계대명사

사람
who(m)
that

사물, 동물
which

He is an actor. + Many people love him. 
→ He is an actor who[whom/that] many people love.
           선행사        목적격 관계대명사절
This is the book. + She borrowed it from the library.
 → This is the book which[that] she borrowed from the library.
             선행사          목적격 관계대명사절
 The dog is very cute. + I saw it every morning.
 → The dog which[that] I saw every morning is very cute.
     선행사          목적격 관계대명사절
• 목적격 관계대명사는 생략할 수 있다. 
The song (which[that]) I heard on the radio is my favorite.

 다음 문장에서 목적격 관계대명사가 들어갈 가장 알맞은 곳을 골라 봅시다.
		(1) She ① is ② a friend ③ I ④ can trust ⑤. 
(2) It ① is ② a problem ③ we ④ need ⑤ to solve. 
(3) The man ① I ② met ③ yesterday ④ was ⑤ very kind. 
(4) She is ① a teacher ② all ③ the students ④ respect ⑤. 
(5) He ① liked ② the cake ③ I made ④ for ⑤ his birthday.

 밑줄 친 부분을 생략할 수 있는 문장을 모두 골라 봅시다. (3개)
		(1) The boy who called you is my brother.
(2) This is the restaurant that I told you about.
(3) The neighbor who she invited didn’t come.
(4) The movie which we watched last night was amazing.
(5) She showed me a painting that was made by her grandfather.
