# 2022me 중2 L5 문법연습문제 심화

Lesson 5                                         2학년 ___반 ___번 이름: ___________
문법 연습문제
         심화
  다음 우리말과 같은 뜻이 되도록 괄호 안의 말을 배열해 봅시다.

1 그녀는 나에게 도와달라고 요청했다. (asked, to help, she, her, me)
  → ______________________________________________________________________.
2 나는 네가 추천한 그 노래를 좋아한다. (recommended, that, I, like, the song, you)
  → ______________________________________________________________________.
3 그 요리사가 만든 요리는 매우 맛있었다. (which, the chef, the dish, was, made, very delicious)
  → ______________________________________________________________________.


  두 문장의 뜻이 같도록 to부정사를 이용하여 문장을 완성해 봅시다. 

1 Lisa said that I should take an umbrella.
  → Lisa advised me _________________________________________.
2 My dad said that I could try the new game.
  → My dad allowed me _________________________________________.
3 The coach said that I must run faster.
  → The coach ordered me _________________________________________.


  다음 두 문장을 목적격 관계대명사를 사용하여 한 문장으로 바꿔 써 봅시다.

1 He showed me the photo. He took the photo in Paris.
  → He showed me the photo ___________ ___________ ___________ in Paris.
2 I found the wallet. You lost the wallet.
  → I found the wallet ___________ ___________ ___________.  
3 The police caught the thief. They were chasing the thief.
  → The police caught the thief ___________ ___________ ___________ ___________.



  다음 대화에서 어법상 틀린 부분을 모두 찾아 바르게 고쳐 써 봅시다.
    
A: I finished the science report. I want you read it.
B: Is it the report who you started on Sunday?
A: Yes, it is.
B: Okay. I’ll read it.

1 _______________ → _______________ 
2 _______________ → _______________ 


<정답>
A 
1 She asked me to help her
2 I like the song that you recommended
3 The dish which the chef made was very delicious
B  
1 to take an umbrella
2 to try the new game 
3 to run faster
C  
1 which[that] he took
2 which[that] you lost
3 who[whom/that] they were chasing
D 
1 read → to read
2 who → which[that]
