         기본 A
문법 연습문제

A. 「make/let/have+목적어+동사원형」

   • 의미: ~가 …하게 하다/만들다 
   • 사역동사는 주어가 목적어에게 어떤 것을 하도록 시키거나 허락하는 동사로, make, let, have가 있다. 
• 「make+목적어+동사원형」: ~가 …하게 하다/만들다
  The storm made everyone stay home.
                모든 사람을 머물게 했다
• 「let+목적어+동사원형」: ~가 …하게 해 주다
  The teacher doesn’t let us use our phones during class.
            우리가 사용하지 못하게 하다
• 「have+목적어+동사원형」: ~가 …하게 하다
  I had my sister help me with the math homework. 
     우리 언니가 돕게 했다

 다음 괄호 안에서 알맞은 말을 골라 봅시다.
		(1) His words made her (cry / to cry).
(2) The coach let him (play / playing) in the game.
(3) The manager had me (preparing / prepare) the event.
(4) The movie made me (think / to think) about my future.
(5) My mom has my brother (set / sets) the table before dinner.

         기본 B
문법 연습문제

B. 「as+형용사/부사의 원급+as」

  
 • 의미: ~만큼 …한[하게]
• 원급을 사용하여 두 대상을 동등하게 비교하는 표현으로, 두 번째 as 뒤에는 비교 대상이 온다.
  My bag is as heavy as yours.
  This apple is as sweet as honey.
  He runs as fast as his friend.
  The baby cried as loudly as the others.                   
• 부정문은 「not as+형용사/부사의 원급+as」의 형태로 쓴다.
  The question was not as easy as the first one.
  She doesn’t speak French as well as Marie.

 비교 구문을 바르게 쓴 것을 골라 봅시다.
		(1) ① I wake up as early my dad. 
   ② I wake up as early as my dad.
(2) ① He speaks as clearly as the teacher. 
   ② He speaks more clearly as the teacher.
(3) ① I’m not as good at math as my sister. 
   ② I’m not as good at math than my sister.
(4) ① This book is not as interesting as that one. 
   ② This book is as not interesting as that one.
(5) ① That chair is as comfortable as a sofa. 
   ② That chair is as the most comfortable as a sofa.
