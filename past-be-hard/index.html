Part 1 · 15문제

문맥을 읽고 was / were 판단하기

선택형(1–8) + 빈칸(9–15) · 앞뒤 문장이 힌트예요
1
📌 이 상자에 쿠키가 있어야 했는데, 열었더니 아무것도 없었다.
The box on the shelf ___ empty. I couldn't find the cookies.
shelf 선반empty 텅 빈
💡 정답: was — The box(단수) → was / 쿠키를 못 찾은 이유 = 상자가 비어 있었다
2
📌 제니는 과학 발표회에서 1등을 했다.
She ___ very proud of herself after winning first prize.
proud 자랑스러운prize 상
💡 정답: was — She(단수) → was / "winning first prize"가 과거 단서
3
📌 우리 팀은 최선을 다했지만 상대팀이 더 많은 골을 넣었다.
All of us ___ disappointed after the game.
disappointed 실망한
💡 정답: were — All of us(복수) → were / 경기에서 진 것이 단서
4
📌 내 강아지는 어제 밥을 한 입도 먹지 않고 바닥에 누워만 있었다.
My dog ___ sick. He didn't eat anything all day.
all day 하루 종일
💡 정답: was — My dog(단수) → was / 밥을 안 먹은 이유 = 아팠기 때문
5
📌 카페에 늦게 도착했더니 앉을 자리를 찾을 수가 없었다.
All the chairs ___ taken. I had to stand.
taken 차지된had to ~해야 했다
💡 정답: were — All the chairs(복수) → were / 서 있어야 했던 이유 = 자리가 모두 찼기 때문
6
📌 도서관에 갔더니 문이 닫혀 있었다. 결국 월요일에 다시 와야 했다.
The library ___ closed on Sunday. I came back on Monday.
closed 문을 닫은came back 다시 왔다
💡 정답: was — The library(단수) → was / 월요일에 다시 온 이유 = 일요일에 닫혀 있었기 때문
7
📌 그 운동화를 사고 싶었지만, 가격표를 보고 다시 선반에 올려놓았다.
The sneakers ___ too expensive. I put them back.
sneakers 운동화expensive 비싼price tag 가격표
💡 정답: were — The sneakers(복수) → were / 다시 내려놓은 이유 = 너무 비쌌기 때문
8
📌 아침에 일어나 보니 방 바닥에 빗물이 고여 있었다. 창문이 밤새 열려 있었던 것이다.
The window ___ open all night. That's why the floor got wet.
all night 밤새floor 바닥wet 젖은
💡 정답: was — The window(단수) → was / 바닥이 젖은 이유 = 창문이 열려 있었기 때문
9
📌 샘은 얼굴이 창백했고 배를 잡고 있었다. 보건 선생님이 부모님께 전화를 했다.
Sam well. The nurse called his parents. (be, 부정형)
pale 창백한stomach 배nurse 보건 교사
💡 정답: was not — Sam(단수, 과거 부정) → was not / wasn't도 정답 / 보건실에 간 것이 단서
10
📌 친구들과 나는 수학여행 얘기를 일주일 내내 했다. 빨리 가고 싶어서 안달이 났었다.
We very excited about the school trip.
excited 신이 난school trip 수학여행
💡 정답: were — We(복수) → were / 일주일 내내 얘기했다 = 그만큼 신이 났다
11
📌 가방을 세 번 뒤졌지만 없었다. 결국 주머니 안에서 찾았다.
My key in my bag. I found it in my pocket. (be, 부정형)
key 열쇠pocket 주머니
💡 정답: was not — My key(단수, 과거 부정) → was not / wasn't도 정답 / 주머니에서 찾은 것이 단서
12
📌 그것은 내 생애 첫 비행이었다. 나는 팔걸이를 꽉 잡고 창문만 바라봤다.
It my first time on an airplane. I was very nervous.
flight 비행armrest 팔걸이nervous 긴장한
💡 정답: was — It(단수, 과거) → was / 팔걸이를 잡고 긴장한 이유 = 처음 타는 비행기
13
📌 엄마는 항상 거짓말을 하지 않고 약속을 꼭 지키는 사람이었다.
My mom an honest person. She never lied to me.
honest 정직한never 절대 ~않다
💡 정답: was — My mom(단수, 과거) → was
14
📌 수업이 끝나자마자 모두 교실을 빠져나갔지만 미아 혼자 선생님 정리를 도왔다.
Mia the only student who stayed to help the teacher.
the only 유일한stayed 남았다
💡 정답: was — Mia(단수, 과거) → was
15
📌 축구팀은 뜨거운 햇볕 아래 두 시간 동안 훈련을 했다.
The players tired and thirsty after practice.
player 선수thirsty 목마른practice 훈련
💡 정답: were — The players(복수, 과거) → were / 두 시간 훈련이 단서
Part 2 · 10문제

짧은 지문 빈칸 채우기

세 편의 짧은 글 — 앞뒤 내용으로 was / were를 판단하세요
16
📖 [소풍] Last Saturday, I went on a picnic with my family. The weather ① ___ perfect — sunny and warm. The sandwiches my mom made were so delicious. The park was full of other families, too.
① The weather perfect — sunny and warm.
picnic 소풍perfect 완벽한
💡 정답: was — The weather(단수, 과거) → was
17
📖 [소풍] Last Saturday, I went on a picnic with my family. The weather was perfect — sunny and warm. The sandwiches my mom made ② ___ so delicious. The park was full of other families, too.
② The sandwiches my mom made so delicious.
sandwich 샌드위치delicious 맛있는
💡 정답: were — The sandwiches(복수, 과거) → were
18
📖 [소풍] Last Saturday, I went on a picnic with my family. The weather was perfect — sunny and warm. The sandwiches my mom made were so delicious. The park ③ ___ full of other families, too.
③ The park full of other families, too.
full of ~로 가득한
💡 정답: was — The park(단수, 과거) → was
19
📖 [힘든 하루] Yesterday ① ___ a really bad day for me. I ② ___ late for school because I missed the bus. The bus ③ ___ early that morning, and I couldn't catch it.
① Yesterday a really bad day for me.
bad day 힘든 날
💡 정답: was — Yesterday(단수, 과거) → was
20
📖 [힘든 하루] Yesterday was a really bad day for me. I ② ___ late for school because I missed the bus. The bus ③ ___ early that morning, and I couldn't catch it.
② I late for school because I missed the bus.
miss 놓치다
💡 정답: was — I(1인칭, 과거) → was / 버스를 놓친 것이 지각의 이유
21
📖 [힘든 하루] Yesterday was a really bad day for me. I was late for school because I missed the bus. The bus ③ ___ early that morning, and I couldn't catch it.
③ The bus early that morning, and I couldn't catch it.
early 일찍catch 따라잡다
💡 정답: was — The bus(단수, 과거) → was / 버스가 일찍 와서 못 탄 것
22
📖 [학교 연극] Last Friday, our class put on a school play. The costumes ① ___ beautiful, and the actors ② ___ very confident on stage. The audience ③ ___ not big — only parents and teachers — but everyone ④ ___ happy with the performance.
① The costumes beautiful.
costume 의상put on ~을 공연하다
💡 정답: were — The costumes(복수, 과거) → were
23
📖 [학교 연극] Last Friday, our class put on a school play. The costumes were beautiful, and the actors ② ___ very confident on stage. The audience was not big — only parents and teachers — but everyone was happy with the performance.
② The actors very confident on stage.
actor 배우confident 자신감 있는stage 무대
💡 정답: were — The actors(복수, 과거) → were
24
📖 [학교 연극] Last Friday, our class put on a school play. The costumes were beautiful, and the actors were very confident on stage. The audience ③ ___ not big — only parents and teachers — but everyone was happy with the performance.
③ The audience big — only parents and teachers. (be, 부정형)
audience 관객
💡 정답: was not — The audience(단수 집합명사, 과거 부정) → was not / wasn't도 정답

everyone 모든 사람performance 공연
